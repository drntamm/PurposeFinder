Understanding Ikigai

Ikigai (生き甲斐) is a Japanese concept that means "a reason for being." It lies at the intersection of:

  • What you love - your passions and interests
  • What you're good at - your skills and strengths
  • What the world needs - your contribution to society
  • What you can be paid for - your professional value

Finding Your Purpose

When these elements align, you discover:

  • Passion - where what you love meets what you're good at
  • Mission - where what you love meets what the world needs
  • Profession - where your skills meet what you can be paid for
  • Vocation - where the world's needs meet what you can be paid for
